# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][cc] as its code of
conduct. By participating in this project — opening issues, submitting pull
requests, commenting in discussions, or any other contribution — you agree to
abide by its terms.

## Reporting

Concerns about behaviour in any project space can be reported privately to the
maintainers at the contact address listed in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md). All
reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Reporters will
not be identified without their explicit consent.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces — the repository, issue tracker,
discussions, and any communication channel where someone is representing the
project — and when an individual is officially representing the community in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards. They
have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject contributions
that do not align with the code, and to ban temporarily or permanently any
contributor for behaviours they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or
harmful.

The full text — including the standards we hold ourselves to and the
enforcement guidelines we follow — lives at the canonical Contributor Covenant
site below.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
